Oracle Data Integrator (ODI) 12c

  • Explain ODI Constraints


ODI models are able to use constraints, such as Keys, References, Conditions and non-null flags on attributes.

Key: has a specific role. Primary and alternate keys identify each record uniquely. Non-unique indexes enable optimized record access.

References: functional link between 2 datastores. Correspons to a foreign key in a relational model. Ex: Product datastore references the company datastore through the customer ID

Conditions and Filters: WHERE-type SQL expression attached to a datastore, used to validate or filter the data in the datastore.
